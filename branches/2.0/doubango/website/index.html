Doubango Telecom

Doubango Telecom is a young Telco company focused on open source projects. We are specialized in NGN technologies (3GPP, TISPAN, Packet Cabel, WiMax, GSMA, RCS-e, IETF...standards), audio/video coding and cloud computing.
Our products include SIP/IMS (VoIP) clients/servers, MCUs, VNC stacks and audio/video codecs. Most of our products are already open sourced.

The One Voice Profile (4G/LTE)


Doubango IMS Framework supports both Voice and SMS over LTE as defined by the One Voice initiative.
For more information, please refer to Voice over IMS profile V1.0.0(2009-11).
